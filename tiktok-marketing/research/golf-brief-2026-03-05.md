## 🏆 Biggest SA Golf Story

**LIV Golf Confirmed for Steyn City, Johannesburg (19-22 March)**

Big news this morning! LIV Golf is coming to South Africa for the first time, and it's happening right here in Johannesburg. Jon Rahm, Cam Smith, Bryson DeChambeau, and Louis Oosthuizen are among the stars heading to Steyn City from 19-22 March.

The event is expected to generate R1 billion for the SA economy. This is massive for local golf - not just for spectators, but for the profile of the sport in South Africa.

**Source:** [SA News](https://www.sanews.gov.za/south-africa/sa-generate-r1-billion-hosting-liv-golf)

## ⛳ SA Player Results & Tour News

- **Nikhil Rama** - Ready for another shot at Joburg Open glory at Houghton Golf Club. Looking to follow in the footsteps of Jayden Schaper and Casey Jarvis who won back-to-back on the DP World Tour this season.
- **Jayden Schaper / Casey Jarvis** - Their DP World Tour wins this season giving local players belief
- **Andrea Pavan** - The DP World Tour pro who fell down an elevator shaft at SA Open has been discharged from hospital. He'll remain in SA for 5 more weeks for rehab before flying home. Wishing him a speedy recovery!
