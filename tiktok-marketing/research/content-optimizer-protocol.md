# Swing Shack Content Optimizer Protocol

## Primary Goal
Use past performance + today's trend signals + hook analysis to generate posts that outperform the last 14 days.

## Inputs Required
1. TikTok/IG performance data (last 10-14 posts)
2. Winning hooks & pillars (from analytics)
3. Trend Hunter output (today's trends)

## Non-Negotiables
- No "AI-sounding" copy
- No corporate language
- No cheesy hyperbole
- Every post must be specific, practical, or properly funny
- No fake promises of outcomes

## Process

### STEP 1: Performance Summary
- Top 3 posts (by Engagement Score)
- Worst 2 posts (why they flopped)
- Best pillar
- Best hook type
- Best posting time window (SAST)

### STEP 2: Hook Bank (data-driven)
Create 10 hooks:
- 4 hooks based on what already wins
- 3 hooks adapted from today's trends
- 3 "new bets" (one variable changed)

Hooks must be short and scroll-stopping.

### STEP 3: Pick Today's Test (one variable only)
Choose ONE:
- Hook type
- Slide count
- CTA
- Tone
- Depth (simple vs technical)
- Format (meme vs checklist vs "numbers prove it")

State: "Today we test: ____ (control vs variation)"

### STEP 4: Generate 3 Posts
For each post:
- Hook (Slide 1 text)
- Slide-by-slide script (6-8 slides max)
- Caption (short)
- Hashtags (5-8)
- CTA (soft: comment/save/send to mate)
- Why it should win

### STEP 5: Recommend #1 Post
Choose best post, justify in 2 bullets.

## Dashboard Compatibility
- Do NOT create new pages
- Do NOT change existing links
- Only update EXISTING social-dashboard.html by appending:
  1. "Optimized Post Ideas" section
  2. "Today's Test Variable" badge
  3. "Hook Bank (Top 10)"
- Keep existing sections intact
