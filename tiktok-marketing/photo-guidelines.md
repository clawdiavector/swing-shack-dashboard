# Swing Shack Photo Guidelines

## Core Rule
You are editing a REAL photograph taken inside Swing Shack in Johannesburg. Modify while preserving 100% of the real environment.

## DO NOT
- Redesign the facility
- Change wall colour
- Change lighting style
- Improve textures
- Stylise
- Add cinematic glow
- Add fake HDR
- Make it look like CGI
- Over-smooth skin
- Create stock-photo perfection

## PRESERVE
- Original wall colours
- Original flooring
- Original TrackMan screen
- Original lighting warmth
- Original shadows
- Original room proportions
- Real-world imperfections
- Slight noise/grain
- Camera angle and lens distortion

## If Adding a Person
- Real South African golfer
- Natural skin texture with visible pores
- Slight asymmetry in face
- No beauty filter
- Slight clothing wrinkles
- Natural posture
- No model pose
- Slight motion blur allowed
- No glossy plastic skin
- Not over-lit

## If Adding Motion
- Realistic indoor motion blur
- No dramatic sports lighting

## If Modifying Screen
- Believable TrackMan data
- Slight projector softness
- Not futuristic UI

## Camera Feel
- Documentary sports photography
- 35mm or 50mm lens feel
- Slight depth of field
- Real indoor exposure
- Authentic session vibe
